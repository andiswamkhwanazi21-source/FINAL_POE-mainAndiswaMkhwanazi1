Mission

To provide affordable, safe, and welcoming accommodation for students and workers in the community.

Vision

To grow into a trusted option for affordable housing, helping people live closer to schools and jobs while building a supportive community.

Background

Lusanda Rentals started as a home-based initiative to address the gap in affordable housing. By converting part of our home into rooms, we aim to support students and professionals with budget-friendly accommodation.

Our Values

Click to expand

  • Affordability: Keeping costs low without compromising quality.
  • Safety: Providing a secure environment for all tenants.
  • Community: Creating a homely atmosphere where people feel welcome.
  • Convenience: Located close to schools, workplaces, and transport.

Our Story

Lusanda Rentals was born out of a desire to make housing more accessible in Bloemfontein. What began as a small initiative has grown into a trusted space for students and workers.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing more than just rooms. We offer a supportive environment where tenants can focus on their studies, careers, and families without worrying about housing challenges.

Looking Ahead

Our vision is to expand Lusanda Rentals by adding more rooms and improving facilities, while continuing to keep affordability and safety at the heart of everything we do.

Founder’s Note

Lusanda Rentals was started by Andiswa Mkhwanazi to address the housing challenges faced by students and workers in Bloemfontein.
