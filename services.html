Room Details

At Lusanda Rentals, we offer rooms tailored to different needs and lifestyles:

  • Single Rooms (En-suite): Perfect for those who value privacy and independence. Each single room comes with its own private bathroom, a comfortable bed, desk, and cupboard — ideal for students or professionals.
  • Sharing for 2: A great option for friends or colleagues who want to live together while keeping costs affordable. Each tenant enjoys their own bed and storage space, with shared access to facilities.
  • Sharing for 3: The most budget-friendly choice, designed for those who enjoy a lively, communal environment. Popular among students and young workers.

All rooms are furnished with essentials and designed to make you feel at home from the moment you arrive.

Facilities

Tenants enjoy shared facilities designed for convenience and safety:

  • Reliable free Wi-Fi
  • Shared kitchen equipped with stove, fridge, and microwave
  • Laundry facilities with washing machines
  • Secure environment with locked gates and safe surroundings

Pricing

We offer flexible options to suit different budgets and living preferences:

  • Single Room (En-suite): R3,000 – R3,300 depending on size
  • Sharing (2 people): R2,600 per person
  • Sharing (3 people): R2,200 per person
