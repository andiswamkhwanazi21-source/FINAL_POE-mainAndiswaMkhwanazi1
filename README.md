## Project Overview
*Lusanda Rentals* is a small rooms‑to‑rent initiative based in Bloemfontein, South Africa. The business provides safe, clean, and affordable accommodation for students, young professionals, and small families.  

The website serves as a digital presence for Lusanda Rentals, showcasing available rooms, facilities, pricing, and nearby attractions. It also enables users to make enquiries and bookings online.  


## Website Goals and Objectives
- *Goal 1: Build a professional online presence*  
  The website aims to establish Lusanda Rentals as a credible and trustworthy accommodation provider. By presenting information clearly and attractively, the site builds confidence among potential tenants and positions the business competitively against other rental options.  

- *Goal 2: Provide accessible information and convenience*  
  The site ensures that visitors can easily find details about room types, facilities, pricing, and nearby attractions. This reduces the need for phone calls or in-person visits, saving time for both tenants and the business owner.  

- *Goal 3: Enable seamless enquiries and bookings*  
  Through enquiry and contact forms, the website allows users to submit booking requests or general questions directly online. JavaScript validation ensures that submissions are accurate, while success messages provide reassurance to users. This functionality streamlines communication and improves customer service.  


## Key Features and Functionality
- Five or more web pages (Home, About, Rooms & Services, Enquiry/Booking, Contact).  
- Semantic HTML structure for accessibility.  
- Responsive CSS design for desktop, tablet, and mobile.  
- Enquiry and contact forms with JavaScript validation.  
- Creative JavaScript functionality (animations, modals, rotating testimonials, scroll effects).  
- SEO elements (title, description, keywords, alt text, robots.txt, sitemap.xml).

## Part 1 Details
### Proposal Summary
- *Proposal Option 1: Lusanda Rentals*  
  Lusanda Rentals is a community‑centered housing initiative designed to address the shortage of affordable accommodation in Bloemfontein. The business converts part of a home into clean, budget‑friendly rooms with shared facilities such as Wi‑Fi, kitchen, and laundry. The target audience includes students, young professionals, and small families who need safe, convenient housing close to schools and workplaces.  
  The unique value of Lusanda Rentals lies in its affordability, safety, and welcoming environment. Unlike traditional rentals, Lusanda Rentals emphasizes community living, where tenants feel supported and connected. The website plays a crucial role in promoting this vision by showcasing rooms, facilities, and nearby attractions, while enabling easy enquiries and bookings.
